Top-Notch & Trusted Orthopedic Surgeries

Total Knee Replacement (TKR)

Advanced knee replacement surgery, using mobile-bearing implants, offers long-lasting knee function. Moreover, this procedure helps restore mobility and relieve pain caused by severe arthritis or joint damage.

Partial Knee Replacement

Ideal for patients with early-stage arthritis, therefore, this surgery preserves most of the knee structure, which ensures faster recovery and excellent functional outcomes, while minimizing the need for a full replacement.

Reverse Shoulder Arthroplasty

An advanced shoulder surgery for patients with severe rotator cuff damage. As a result, this procedure allows for improved shoulder function and pain relief, even in complex shoulder cases.

Arthroscopy (Minimally Invasive Surgery)

A minimally invasive procedure used to diagnose and treat joint issues. Moreover, arthroscopy offers faster recovery times, less pain, and more precise treatment for knee, shoulder, and other joint problems.

Total Hip Replacement

A complete hip replacement procedure that effectively replaces damaged hip joints with durable implants, thereby restoring flexibility and reducing pain, ultimately allowing patients to regain their active lifestyles pain-free.
