About Dr. Ankur Saurav

Dr. Ankur Saurav is a highly skilled and best bone specialist doctor in Bariatu Road Ranchi, Jharkhand, with years of experience. His focus includes knee, hip, joint replacement surgery, and should replacements, which allows him to balance care with modern surgical practices so that his patients can become more mobile and have a better life.
Academic Excellence
It all began when Dr. Ankur earned an MBBS degree (Gold Medalist) at the Eras Lucknow Medical College; all these exceptional academic surroundings made it certain that he would pursue yet another diploma in orthopedics at Kolkata Medical College, where he placed 1st in the West Bengal University of Health Sciences. His goal of excellence in mind led him to do his DNB in orthopedics from the Tata Main Hospital in Jamshedpur, where he placed 8th in the All India rankings. Dr. Ankur has completed Arthroplasty (Germany) and Sports (ISAKOS) fellowships to upgrade his knowledge. Innovative Expertise
Numerous people in the medical field respect Dr. Ankur for doing surgeries differently. He is among the handful of surgeons present in India who are involved in performing complicated operations, which include:
Rotating Platform Knee Replacements
Partial Knee Replacements
Reverse Shoulder Replacements
He is notable for being the first surgeon to complete a Reverse Shoulder Arthroplasty in Jharkhand.
Working in several renowned national and international hospitals has given Dr. Ankur ample opportunities to present papers on knee Biomech.
