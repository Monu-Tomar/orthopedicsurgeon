Bone density testing, also known as DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) Scan, is a non-invasive and painless procedure used to measure the strength and density of your bones. It is crucial in diagnosing osteoporosis and assessing the risk of fractures, particularly in older adults and individuals with a family history of bone-related conditions.
